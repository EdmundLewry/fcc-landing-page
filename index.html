Wander On

Bring the spirit of adventure to your town or city with Wander On, an exploration app for your Android phone or tablet! Wander On provides a map that shows where you've been, uncovering it as you go and highlighting places of interest for you to strive towards. Find something new? Add it to your map.

Free and open source, Wander On let's you explore secure in the knowledge that your location data is yours alone.

Bring a sense of wonder to your world and Wander On!

Download maps of your local area to use offline, and uncover the world around you. Become intimately familiar with the area you love, or venture forth and uncover new places.

Log walking or running routes or download the routes that others have uploaded.

Add notes to the things you discover and upload them for others to find!
